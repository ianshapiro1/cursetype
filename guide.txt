Welcome to CurseType, a customizable TUI typing test game.
The game measures wpm and accuracy as you type, then tells
you the amount of Missed(M), Fixed(F), Incorrect(I), and
Correct(C) letters upon completion.

Commands:
"help" --show this guide
"sentence" or "s" --generate a random sentence typing test
"paragraph" or "p" --generate a random paragraph typing test
"settings" or "c" --change the colors or vocabulary
"quit" or "q" --quit the game

Close(home)
